Get All text Between two string (Data can be multiple line with empty sapces and empty lines)

1 : Regex

(?=Date Timekeeper)[\S\s]*(?=Fee Total)    (This (?=) will include Date Timekeeper)

(?<=Date Timekeeper)[\S\s]*(?=Fee Total)    (This (?<=) will exclude Date Timekeeper)

2: Replace Between Text 

System.Text.RegularExpressions.Regex.Replace(GetText,"(?=Taxpayer Identification Number)[\S\s]*(?<=202\d)","")  

 (This (?<=) will include 2022)
